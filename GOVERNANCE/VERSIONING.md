## Versioning Rules
1. **Major Version (v2.x.x):** Fundamental changes to the legal philosophy or core technical markers.
2. **Minor Version (vx.1.x):** New tooling, improved heuristics, or expanded documentation.
3. **Patch Version (vx.x.1):** Minor bug fixes in SDKs or clarification of definitions.
