**Version:** 1.1  
**Status:** Active Specification  
**Category:** Author-Defined Licensing Protocol

---

## What is RCF?

RCF (Restricted Correlation Framework) is an **author-defined licensing protocol** designed to protect intellectual property in the age of automated extraction and AI/ML mass replication.

### Core Principle: Visibility ≠ Rights

RCF creates a clear legal and technical boundary between:
- **Visibility** (seeing/reading the code) — Allowed
- **Usage Rights** (using/applying the methodology) — Restricted

---

## Why RCF?

Traditional open-source licenses fail to protect against:
- 🤖 AI/ML training on proprietary methodologies
- 🕷️ Automated extraction of correlation logic
- 📊 Mass replication without attribution or compensation
- 💼 Commercial exploitation of research investments

RCF addresses these gaps through a **layered protection model**.
