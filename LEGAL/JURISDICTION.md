# RCF-JURISDICTION — International Applicability

**Version:** 2.0  

---

## 1. Governing Law

Unless otherwise specified in a project's `NOTICE.md`, the RCF protocol and its licenses are interpreted under the laws of the jurisdiction where the primary Author resides.

## 2. International Enforcement

RCF utilizes international treaties, including:
- **WIPO Copyright Treaty (WCT):** For protection in the digital environment.
- **Berne Convention:** For universal copyright recognition.
- **TRIPS Agreement:** For intellectual property enforcement standards.

## 3. Severability

If any provision of an RCF license is found to be invalid or unenforceable under local law, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
