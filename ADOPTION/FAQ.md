# RCF-FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

### Is RCF Open Source?
Technically, no. RCF is "Visible Source". It doesn't meet the OSD definition because it restricts field-of-use (specifically AI training and commercial replication).

### Why block AI training?
To prevent the de-valuation of human research through automated mass-replication.

### Can I use RCF for my library?
It is recommended for end-user tools and research methodologies, not for foundational infrastructure libraries.
